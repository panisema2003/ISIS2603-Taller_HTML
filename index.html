The Nissan GT-R


The GT-R is an sports car from the japanese brand Nissan. It was firstly introduced on december 2007 in Japan, July 2008 in USA and march 2009 on the rest of the world. However, Nissan introduced this GT-R term on their previous car, the Skyline in 1969 with the 'PGC-10', and from 1996 to 2002 the R33 and R34 Skyline GT-R's where created, quite posibly the most iconic GT-R's of them all.
